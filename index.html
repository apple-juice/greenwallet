    What is it?

    Manifesto

    Why Green Energy Wallet?

    Two significant emerging technologies will determine our future: renewable energies and electric vehicles. The total carbon footprint of an electric vehicle is already 50 percent less than that of an internal combustion engine, even considering that many of the energy sources that we use to generate the vehicle’s electricity are not themselves renewable. Were the same vehicle to utilize renewable energy only, it would leave a mere 2 percent of the total carbon footprint of today’s internal combustion engine. Clearly, promising gains stand to be made in this area. But renewable are not as regular as conventional power plants and electricity cannot be stored in the grid: To ensure that the transition away from fossil fuels is a practical and achievable goal, we need efficient facilities to store renewable energy.

    Our Green Energy Wallet combines the unused battery capacity of many electric vehicles with an intelligent system to use this capacity as a large buffer store for renewable energy. Solar power systems and home battery storages can also be connected.

    We are developing a bi-directional charging system for a smart grid that enables electricity to flow either way between the power grid and any electric vehicle or home battery connected to the Green Energy Wallet.

    What is Green Energy Wallet?

    The Green Energy Wallet system has two interrelated components:

    01.
    The Green Energy Wallet app

    The Green Energy Wallet app is the heart of our solution. It provides the software connection between the electric vehicle and the Green Energy Wallet Power Box. With this app, the user can charge or discharge an electric vehicle based on typical preferences or current needs. The app also provides other useful functions, such as access to car settings; statistics on mileage, consumption, and driving style; and a HUD display featuring the vehicle’s current charge level and GPS location.

    02.
    The Intelligent Green Energy Wallet Power Box

    The intelligent Green Energy Wallet Power Box is a hardware component that supports the rapid charging and discharging of an electric vehicle’s battery. A bi-directional meter measures the flow of electricity to and from the Power box . The user can also connect a home battery storage or any private energy production such as a solar power systemt and transfer renergy to where it is needed at any given time.

    Key benefits

    For Green Energy Wallet:

    • Green Energy Wallet is profiting from energy trading on the energy market by charging energy at off-peak and sell it at peak periods.
    • The solution can provide emergency power backup during outages.
    • Green Energy Wallet is using existing battery as energy storage; there are not expensive installation and maintenance costs.


    For Green Energy Wallet users:

    • Green Energy Wallet is taking over of their battery costs.
    • Charge their electric vehicle cheaper.
    • Draw from energy stored in their electric vehicle battery on off-peak times during peak hours
    • The Green Energy Wallet App provides many useful functions for users.
    • Replacing and recycling of their battery at the end of battery life.
    • Extend of the battery life by managing and regulating charge/discharge cycles.
    • EV owners can use the electric vehicle battery instead of an home battery storage as a supplement to their solar power system.
    • Green Energy Wallet users make a significant contribution to environmental protection.


    For electricity providers:

    • Electricity providers can draw on the energy in connected batteries to meet instantaneous energy demands.
    • The installation von expensive energy storage facilities with negative influence on the environment are not necessary anymore.
    • The Green Energy Wallet system can provide emergency power backup during outages.
